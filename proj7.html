“..and Counting”

Digital Video

Original and found video footage

2:46

2018



Time is a currency. Are you spending it wisely? There is difference between wasting time, and things that take a long time to do. Is time spent well if you enjoy it? Does that make exceptions when society, as a whole, thinks it’s a waste of time. This video allows self reflection, allows the audience to think about how much time they're spending, where, and how. I focused on the internet for this video, I do want to expand and also bring in things like being in your car/traffic, or shopping ETC.
